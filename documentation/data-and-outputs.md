## Setup-generated artifacts

Running setup mode (`singlerun -Setup` or `multirun -DBSetup`) creates or refreshes:

- `input/input.mv.db` вЂ” H2 database of EUROMOD donor tax-benefit outcomes
- `input/EUROMODpolicySchedule.xlsx` вЂ” maps simulation years to EUROMOD policy systems
- `input/DatabaseCountryYear.xlsx` вЂ” country- and year-specific macro parameters

These three files must exist before any simulation run. If they are missing, re-run setup.

## Logging

With `-f` on `multirun.jar`, logs are written to:

- `output/logs/run_<seed>.txt` вЂ” stdout capture
- `output/logs/run_<seed>.log` вЂ” log4j output
